What about me?

Miguel Angel Romero Parra


I was born on December 29, 2005




At the age of 8, I demonstrated a passion for programming when I observed my brother coding. This caught my attention, and I began to imitate him. My first programming language was Java, although I mainly copied and pasted what I saw in videos. That's how my journey began. At the age of 15, I returned with even more passion, now aware of the vast world of development. I started with HTML and CSS; however, Python, Java, and JavaScript caught my interest. These three languages ultimately led me to choose a career in Software Engineering.

In this field, I acquired strong foundational knowledge and started working in teams. I often took the lead, assigning tasks and guiding the team on how to approach them. Nevertheless, I never left a teammate struggling with a piece of code. Instead, I would sit down with them, explain concepts, and offer ideas to help them complete the task. Through these actions and my personality, I earned the trust and affection of many colleagues.

For this reason, I enjoy being in constant learning and challenge, as I get easily bored if I feel I'm not making the most of my time. I like being in continuous personal and social development.
